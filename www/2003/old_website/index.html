Jay Hannah will be taking over the care and feeding of the Omaha Perl Mongers, this web site, and the mailing list. We are in the process of getting things set up so he can begin maintaining things ... check back soon!

To subscribe to the Omaha Perl Mongers mailing list, send email to majordomo@pm.org with the following line in the body of your message:

subscribe omaha-list

You will be sent a confirmation code to return by email in order for your address to be actually put on the list. Once you are confirmed, send email to omaha-list@pm.org to post messages to your fellow mongers.
