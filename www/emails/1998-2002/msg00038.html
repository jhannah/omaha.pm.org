Remember, the Omaha Perl Mongers are meeting tonight (Wednesday, April
21st at 6:30 pm) at HDR on 84th and Dodge (courtesy Kent Tegels and HDR,
Inc.). Kent will be giving a presentation entitled "Teaching a new dog
old tricks: Perl on the Win32 platform", and will have an open question
and answer forum following that (time permitting).

Directions to the meeting at HDR (see http://www.tegels.org:8080/perl/
as well) :

HDR is located just south of 84th and West Dodge Road. I suggest
approaching our parking lot from 84th street.

If going south on 84th, enter via left turn at the last driveway BEFORE
the lights at Indian Hills Drive.

If going north on 84th, enter via left turn at the first driveway AFTER
the lights at Indian Hills Drive.

Park in the gold-colored glass build's parking lot (East end). Just look
for the porta-potties. Enter the gold (East) building from the North
revolving door, going south down the short hallway to the two elevators.

Take the elevator to third floor, and immediately exit to your right. Go
south until you are in the East/West hallway. Turn right (West) and go
down this hallway. About 20 feet down this path you will see a room on
your right marked "AV Room." Enter. 

Got that?

Hope to see you there!
