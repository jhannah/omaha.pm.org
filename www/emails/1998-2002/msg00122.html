I figured it out. In case anyone ever needs it:

$text =~ s/(http:\/\/[A-Za-z0-9%.=+&?\/]+[A-Za-z0-9%=+&?\/])/
           <A HREF=\"$1\">$1<\/A>/g;

This fixes the problem of trailing commas and periods being linked.
