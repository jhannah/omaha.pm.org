As you can infer, we had a problem with the server that hosts all things
'monger' last week ... things are slowly coming back ... a rebuild from
tape backup was done on the web sites ... other things are being
re-built and re-configured as we speak. I hope the interruption to
service for this list wasn't too much of a burden! :) ... so *many*
messages sent and lost, I'm sure :)

Speaking of which, someone posted 10 days ago, or so, about having students ask
questions to this list. Well I tried to respond, but because of how I had it
configured, the majordomo thought I was asking an administrative question, and
didn't forward it to the list. When trying to modify the majordomo over the
weekend, I discovered that the host machine was down. Well I have since been
able to reset the configuration, so this email should now be able to get through
to the list. Just in case you're curious, the dirty word was I had in the
original email (and this one) was 'subscribe' ... the 'administrivia' feature in
majordomo saw this and said "oh my! someone is trying to subscribe to the
discussion list ... I won't post it there, and instead I'll notify the list
owner of this silly mistake". So everytime I tried to post it it bounced. It
took me 3 attempts before I figured it out.

So I guess in a head-to-head, one-on-one clash of wits between me and a Perl
Script (which is what majordomo is), I uh .... ummmmmmmmmm ... come in second.

Yeah! That's the ticket ... I made it to the finals, and finished a respectable
second place!

This list is an open, private, unmoderated list created to enhance the
following: 

  Discussion of Perl 
  Discussion of things related to Perl 
  Discussion of the Omaha Perl Mongers and their activities 

The list is currently open to all ... not just actual Omaha Perl Mongers
... this may or may not change in the future. 

To subscribe to the list, send email to majordomo@xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx
with the following blurb on it's own line in the body of your message
(minus the quotes): 'subscribe omaha-list'

That's it! You will be sent a confirmation code to return by email in
order for your address to be actually put on the list. Once you are
confirmed, send email to omaha-list@xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx to post messages
to your fellow mongers. 

Please feel free to contact me with any questions or problems you have
in regard to subscribing to the list. 

Contact geek@xxxxxxxxxxxx with any questions you might have regarding
the Omaha Perl Mongers or the mailing list.
