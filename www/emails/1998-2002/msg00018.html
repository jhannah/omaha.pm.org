I have to get my notes posted to the web-site ... sorry ...
as far as how it went ... Jeffrey Friedl has nothing to worry
about :(

I was stunned at how difficult regexes are to explain ...
people had mentioned this in various threads on comp.lang.perl.misc,
but I pretty much pooh-poohed it ... *I* would be able to do it,
since *I* understand them pretty thoroughly ... HA! HA! HA!

I found it virtually impossible to explain one concept without
introducing another (as of yet, undefined) concept ... so I was
constantly veering off from my original tack to explain the
new concept, then veering off *that* tack to explain *another* concept,
etc, etc, etc :(

Fortunately, no one fell *completely* asleep ... meaning no one actually
laid their head down on the table. But I know that I have reached a
state where I can get a pretty good rest by just closing my eyes ...
my head doesn't nod or anything ... so you never know :)

Bud Uesu hit the nail on the head at the meeting ... what we need
to do is just sit down informally and chat about Perl ... people
can bring in their problems, or examples of things they are trying 
to design in Perl, then we can all discuss them in a 'round-table'
fashion, demonstrating various concepts of Perl as they arise.

This sounds like a winner to me!
