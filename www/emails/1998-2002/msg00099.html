Fellow Mongers,

	I'm currently teaching a "Programming on the Internet" class at UNO (CSCI
2850).  The class basically covers Perl and Java, though most of what we'll
probably cover will be Perl since Java is likely to be a course of it's own in
the next semester or two.

	ANYWAY, I've had some student inquiries as to whether or not there were any
human resources out there who would be interested in helping answer questions
about Perl for their assignments.

	I don't want to point them directly to the list en masse, because this list
isn't meant to be a student assignment forum.  What I'm wondering is if there
is anyone out there who would be interested in possibly fielding some e-mail
Perl questions.  I'm available during office hours and via e-mail, but
sometimes it just helps to have an extra brain or four to solve a problem.

	I'm _not_ looking to find people to write programs for my students -- and I
don't think I really have to worry about that happening.  But it would be cool
to find some people who really want to get others just as excited about Perl
as we are.

	If you're interested, or if you would be interested in coming to talk about
some aspect of Perl (maybe about how you use it at work) for 20 or 30 minutes,
please feel free to drop me a line at robert@xxxxxxxxxxxxxx  I'll see if
there's any interest and then we can work out the specifics.

	Thanks!
