Thanks to the efforts of Wyatt Draggoo, the Omaha Perl Mongers will be
meeting next week:

  Who: Everyone is welcome
 What: Omaha Perl Mongers Meeting
Where: Last Avenue Computers
 When: Tuesday, July 18 6:30 pm
  Why: Not?
  How: See the directions below

There won't be any formal topic.  Bring in your Perl questions or
puzzlers and see if anyone can offer some insight.  Or if you have
something (Perl related) that you'd like to talk about, just come on in
and start talkin'

Hope to see you there!  Tell all your friends!

Directions to Last Avenue Computers:

Re-interating Wyatt's directions, Last Avenue Computers is located at
2100 Harvell Dr. in Bellevue (the corner of Harvell Dr. and Herman). 
Cornhusker becomes Harvell Dr. at Fort Crook Road.  So if you're on the
Interstate, you can just take the Kennedy Freeway to the Cornhusker
exit.  Then take Cornhusker east.  Shortly after crossing Fort Crook
Road, you will see Last Avenue Computers on the south side of the street
... right in front of the Big K-Mart.
