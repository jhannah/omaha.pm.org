BTW,

bootstrap@xxxxxxxx is a mailing list created a week or two ago. It's
being used by the Perl 6 developers to flesh out what needs to be done
to make Perl 6 a reality.  You can find more info at the official Perl 6
developement page at:

http://www.perl.org/perl6/

You can subscribe to the bootstrap mailing list by sending an empty
message to:

bootstrap-subscribe@xxxxxxxx

You will be sent back a confirmation message, which you must reply to in
order to begin receiving the list messages.  Be warned though ... very
high volume at last check.
