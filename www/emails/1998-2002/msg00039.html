At the meeting last week, Matt Payne asked if the Perl Cookbook was
available in HTML format ... well O'Reilly must have had many similar
requests, because they just anounced the "The Perl CD Bookshelf" ...
which contains 'The Perl Cookbook', plus 'Perl in a Nutshell',
'Programming Perl', 'Advanced Perl Programming', 'Learning Perl', and
'Learning Perl on Win32 Systems' ... the entire texts ... plus indexes
for each, and a master index for the entire 6 book library. It's on
CD-ROM and all in HTML for viewing with your browser. The cost from
O'Reilly is $59.95, and they also throw in a hard copy of 'Perl in a
Nutshell' ... their newest Perl text. This 'Payne' guy has alot of pull,
I guess.

Thanks again to Kent Tegels and HDR for last weeks presentation and
hospitality ... who's next? ... any takers? ...
