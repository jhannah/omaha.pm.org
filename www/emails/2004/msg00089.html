Boo.

perl -e 'die "argh!"'
argh! at -e line 1.
perl -e 'die "argh!\n"'
argh!

See how adding \n to a die suppresses the line number and program name from the die output? That's bad and the \n serves no purpose I am aware of.

Let's avoid this. (I've been happily stripping it out of Justin's code every time I hit it.)
