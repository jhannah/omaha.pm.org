Hi Dave,

You could do it a couple of ways (I am sure there are more).

/^[A-Za-z][A-Za-z][A-Za-z]$/
/^[A-Za-z]{3,3}$/

$ cat r
ABC
DEF
ABCDEF

$ perl -n -e 'print "found ($1)\n" if(/^([A-Za-z]{3,3})$/)' < r
found (ABC)
found (DEF)

Hope that helps,
George.
