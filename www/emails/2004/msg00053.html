Language holy wars.  Ah, the memories.....
Outside of a personal vow never to write another line of COBOL again,  I 
really have no axe to grind with other languages.   I've written BASIC, PL/I, 
COBOL, FORTRAN, some baby perl, and lots of shell.  I'm still interested in 
learning PHP and C++, but there are so many cool things to do in the 
universe, and so little time.   
I personally think perl has enough momentum to be around for quite a long 
time.   And I still giggle when I think 3-4 years ago at the start of "java 
hype", one of my co-workers saw my Camel book and asked me "What do you want 
to learn that for?! It's a dead language!"
