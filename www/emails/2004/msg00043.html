I spent my morning mucking with LWP::UserAgent.  It's quite cool -- not that hard to
do, and better than using curl/wget and then reading in a file.

A perldoc LWP::UserAgent will show all you need.  I needed to use a username/pass to
get into the page I was interested, and this page is a good example of it:

http://chinto.hypermart.net/lwp/LWP_Credentials.html

So throw out those system calls!  LWP is the way to go!
