Finally spoke to our "T-shirt Contact" and found out that we can do
other colors only if we have 6 or more of that color.  Another catch is
that the color of the print is "white" on the black shirts.  If someone
wants to go with a lighter colored shirt, we'll have to change the ink
color (like to black or something) and there is a $20 charge per ink
change.

send you our info (once I collect the color specific info).  Just so we
have once spot to look at when determining which colors make the cut and
if we'll need to do an ink change or not.
