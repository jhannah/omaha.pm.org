On Apr 27, 2004, at 12:04 PM, omaha.pm.knitter@xxxxxxxxxxxx wrote:
Chomp removes the newline, but won't remove the \r.

I don't think that's right. I believe chomp() removes "\n" on Unix, "\r\n" on Windows.

perldoc -f chomp

99% of the time you should find that chomp() is all you need.

The exception is if your file is from Windows and you're processing on Unix (or vice versa). In that case, I've done this before:

s/[\r\n]+$//;
