On Friday, January 2, 2004, at 09:18 AM, Bob McCoy wrote:
Jay, please excuse me for not taking notes at previous meetings, but I
would like to load a CPAN module and I know I've seen you give a command
line to do it. For instance, I need the Net::Whois::IANA module. How
do I get it?

To activate the shell:

perl -MCPAN -e 'shell'

then "install Net::Whois::IANA".

Or, all in 1 step:

perl -MCPAN -e 'install Net::Whois::IANA'

perldoc CPAN
