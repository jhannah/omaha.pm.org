For those of you who are fans of the ActiveState Perl bits, I'd thought
you'd like to know that ActiveState announced this morning that they've been
gobbled up by Sophos, an Anti-Spam firm from the UK. ActiveState will
basically give Sophos PureMail, but will keep Komodo and the language
stacks. Its hard for me to understand how ActiveState will keep their lights
on for long after this. PureMail seemed to be their best (if not only)
meaningful source of revenue. Lets hope they do.
