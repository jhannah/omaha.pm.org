Summer fun, geek style:
2004 O'Reilly Open Source Convention
July 26-30, 2004
Portland Marriott Downtown, Portland, OR
http://conferences.oreilly.com/oscon/

We've just set the date for the sixth annual O'Reilly Open Source
Convention and we hope you'll be able to join us next summer.
We had a great time in Portland, and can't wait to get back! Fresh
ideas are already sizzling on the OSCON program barbie, including
a keynote address by one of 2003's most riveting speakers, Robert
Lefkowitz. So start planning your trip, because next year's
conference is a must-do event. And bring the family along--Portland
has something for everyone.

Keep an eye out for the Call for Participation--we'll be sending that
out come December. OSCON registration opens in April 2004.
