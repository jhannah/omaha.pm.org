> Question:
> How can I access/change the first N letters of a string?
> 
>     There are many ways. If you just want to grab a copy, use substr():
> 
>         $first_byte = substr($a, 0, 1);
> 
>     If you want to modify part of a string, the simplest way is often
>     to use substr() as an lvalue:
> 
>         substr($a, 0, 3) = "Tom";
> 
>     Although those with a pattern matching kind of thought process will
>     likely prefer:
> 
>         $a =~ s/^.../Tom/;
>
