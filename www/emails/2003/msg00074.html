Hi Craig --

There's a lot of ways you can get help, depending on the scope and depth
of what you're looking for. You may want to join our mailing list:

Omaha Perl Mongers: http://omaha.pm.org/

and come to our meetings for a jump start. We do it just for fun. If you
want formal training I think there are classes at Metro, or you can hire
someone to come out: 

One Perl homepage: http://www.perl.com/
One training option: http://training.perl.com/
More training options: http://www.pm.org/invite/index.html
More? (same?): http://www.perl.org/phbs/training.html

Or you could pay someone local to help you out for a day or whatever...

Of course, there's always Internet resources:
- Usenet comp.lang.perl.*: 
 
http://groups.google.com/groups?hl=en&lr=&ie=UTF-8&group=comp.lang.perl
- EFFnet #perl (IRC chat)
- http://www.perlmonks.org

And plenty of good books. Start w/ Learning Perl or Programming Perl
depending on how language savvy you already are...

That's just off the top of my head. -grin-

I hope that helps,
