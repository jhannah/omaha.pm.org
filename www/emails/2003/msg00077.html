A tip I sent to dbi-users@xxxxxxxx today:



> my $source_tbl = "c:\\somedir\\sometable.txt";
> my $fld_delimiter = "\|";
> my $query = "LOAD DATA INFILE \"$source_tbl\" INTO My_Table " .
>  "FIELDS TERMINATED BY \'$fld_delimiter\'";
> $stmt_handler = $dbh->prepare( $query );
> print "\$query is $query...\n";
> if (!$stmt_handler) { die "Illegal query: $query" }; # doesn't die
> $stmt_handler->execute;
> $stmt_handler->finish;

Perhaps:

my $source_tbl = 'c:\somedir\sometable.txt';
$dbh->do(
   "LOAD DATA INFILE '$source_tbl' INTO My_Table " .
   "FIELDS TERMINATED BY '|' "
) or die $DBI::errstr;
