> If you want to test a string to make sure it's "ABC" or "123" or "XYZ",
> do not use this:
>
>    /ABC|123|XYZ/
>
> and do not use this:
>
>    /^ABC|123|XYZ$/
>
> This is what you should use:
>
>    /^(ABC|123|XYZ)$/

Do not do this either. :)

  /^[ABC|123|XYZ]$/


Everything between the '[' and the ']' is taken as a literal.  So this
would really equate to a search of /^[ABC123XYZ|]$/
