Attendance was up a staggering 25% this meeting! Thanks to Tim, Paul,
Chris, and Bob for coming out to our 2nd 2003 Perl Mongers meeting. We
laughed! We cried! We quoted www.homestarrunner.com again. It was better
than "Cats."

With such an amazing increase in attendance, I took it upon myself as
the defacto Ways and Means Committee chairman to run a
quickextrapolation of our future attendance through 2010. Starting with
last month, with monthly 25% growth here is our projected attendance:

---
#!/usr/bin/perl

use Date::Calc qw( Add_Delta_YMD );

my $a = 4;                 # number of attendees
my ($y, $m) = (2003, 4);   # year, month

while ($y < 2011) {
   print "$y-$m:$a ";
   $a = int($a * 1.25);
   ($y, $m) = Add_Delta_YMD($y, $m, 1, 0, 1, 0);
}
---
